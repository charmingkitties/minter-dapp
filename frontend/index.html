  • Female, brazilian, passionate about animals, traveler and independent artist, I consider myself an NFT enthusiast. I am an immigrant living abroad for the past 5 years, it could be very challenging but creating this collection also brings me an opportunity to be closer to my family after all those years. As mentioned above I am also passionate about animals and I have a rescued cat that helps me go through all these challenges, having a pet brings me joy to keep fighting for my dreams.
